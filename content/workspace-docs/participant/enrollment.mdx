# Joining Challenges

Welcome! This guide will help you discover challenges, enroll, and start participating in your workspace.

## Discovering Challenges

### Browse Available Challenges

View all open challenges in your workspace:

1. Log in to your workspace
2. Navigate to the **Dashboard** or **Challenges** page
3. Browse the list of available challenges

Each challenge card shows:
- **Title and Description**: What the challenge is about
- **Timeline**: Start and end dates
- **Rewards**: What you can earn
- **Status**: Open, Upcoming, or Closed

### Filter and Search

Find specific challenges:

- **By Status**: Open, Upcoming, Completed
- **By Category**: If your workspace uses categories
- **By Keyword**: Search challenge titles and descriptions

## Enrollment Process

### How to Enroll

Join a challenge in three simple steps:

1. **Review the Details**
   - Read the full challenge description
   - Check requirements and submission guidelines
   - Note important dates and deadlines

2. **Click "Enroll"**
   - Find the enrollment button on the challenge page
   - Confirm you understand the requirements

3. **Get Started**
   - You're now enrolled!
   - Check your email for confirmation
   - Begin working on your submission

### Enrollment Confirmation

After enrolling, you'll receive:

- ✅ Email confirmation with challenge details
- ✅ Access to submission forms
- ✅ Notifications about important updates
- ✅ Deadline reminders

## Understanding Requirements

### Submission Guidelines

Each challenge has specific requirements. Common elements include:

**Required Information:**
- Title or project name
- Description of your idea or solution
- Supporting documentation

**Optional Attachments:**
- Images or screenshots
- Documents or presentations
- Links to prototypes or demos
- Videos explaining your submission

**Formatting Tips:**
- Be clear and concise
- Use headings and bullet points
- Proofread before submitting
- Follow any word or file size limits

### Evaluation Criteria

Understand how submissions are judged:

- **Impact**: How significant is your solution?
- **Feasibility**: Can it be implemented?
- **Creativity**: Is it innovative?
- **Clarity**: Is it well-presented?

Specific criteria vary by challenge, so always review the challenge description carefully.

## Participation Tips

### Start Early

Don't wait until the last minute:

1. **Brainstorm**: Generate ideas early
2. **Draft**: Create a rough version
3. **Refine**: Improve and polish
4. **Submit**: Leave time for technical issues

### Ask Questions

If something is unclear:

- Check the challenge FAQ
- Contact workspace admins
- Review examples (if provided)
- Discuss with other participants (if allowed)

### Save Your Work

Protect your progress:

- Draft your submission in a document first
- Save frequently
- Keep backups of attachments
- Note submission deadlines in your calendar

## Multiple Challenges

### Joining Several Challenges

You can participate in multiple challenges simultaneously:

- Each enrollment is independent
- Manage submissions separately
- Track deadlines for each challenge
- Prioritize based on your interests and capacity

### Time Management

Balance multiple challenges:

1. **Review all deadlines**: Create a master calendar
2. **Assess requirements**: Estimate time for each
3. **Prioritize**: Focus on your strongest ideas first
4. **Set milestones**: Break work into manageable chunks

## Enrollment Status

### Checking Your Status

View your enrollment status at any time:

- **Enrolled**: You're registered and can submit
- **Submitted**: You've completed your submission
- **Under Review**: Admins are evaluating your work
- **Approved**: Congratulations! Your submission was accepted
- **Pending Changes**: Revisions requested

### Unenrolling

If you need to withdraw from a challenge:

1. Go to the challenge page
2. Click **Manage Enrollment**
3. Select **Withdraw**
4. Confirm your decision

**Note:** Some challenges may not allow unenrollment after a certain date.

## Getting Help

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I enroll after the start date?**
A: Yes, as long as the enrollment period is still open and you can meet the submission deadline.

**Q: What if I can't complete my submission?**
A: No penalty for not submitting, but you won't be eligible for rewards.

**Q: Can I edit my submission after submitting?**
A: This depends on the challenge settings. Some allow edits until the deadline; others don't.

**Q: How will I know if I'm selected?**
A: You'll receive an email notification and see the status update in your dashboard.
