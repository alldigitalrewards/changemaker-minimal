# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Changemaker.

## General

### What is Changemaker?

Changemaker is a platform for managing innovation challenges, engaging participants, and distributing rewards. It's designed for organizations that want to drive innovation and recognize contributors.

### How much does it cost?

Pricing varies based on your organization's needs. Contact our sales team at [sales@changemaker.im](mailto:sales@changemaker.im) for a custom quote.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required.

## Workspaces

### What is a workspace?

A workspace is an isolated environment for managing challenges and participants. Each workspace has its own:
- Challenges and participants
- Custom branding
- Reward configurations
- Access controls

### Can I have multiple workspaces?

Yes, you can create multiple workspaces to organize different programs or departments.

### How do I invite team members?

Navigate to **Settings â†’ Team** and click **Invite Member**. Enter their email and assign a role (Admin or Participant).

## Challenges

### How many challenges can I create?

There's no limit! Create as many challenges as you need for your programs.

### Can challenges have deadlines?

Yes, you can set start and end dates for challenges. Enrollments are automatically closed when a challenge ends.

### Can I edit a challenge after publishing?

Yes, you can edit challenge details at any time. However, changes won't affect existing enrollments unless you explicitly update them.

### What types of challenges are supported?

Changemaker supports various challenge types:
- Innovation challenges
- Idea submissions
- Problem-solving contests
- Skill-building programs
- Community initiatives

## Participants

### How do participants join?

Participants can:
1. Receive email invitations
2. Use a public enrollment link
3. Browse available challenges in the workspace

### Can participants join multiple challenges?

Yes, participants can enroll in any number of challenges they're eligible for.

### How do I track participant progress?

Use the admin dashboard to monitor:
- Enrollment status
- Submission dates
- Completion rates
- Reward distribution

## Rewards

### What is RewardStack?

RewardStack is our integrated rewards platform that handles:
- Gift card distribution
- Digital rewards
- Points systems
- Payout tracking

### How are rewards distributed?

Rewards are automatically sent to participants via email when you issue them through the platform.

### What reward types are available?

Common options include:
- Gift cards (Amazon, Visa, etc.)
- Digital experiences
- Custom rewards
- Points/credits

### Can I customize reward amounts?

Yes, you can set custom reward amounts when configuring your SKUs in RewardStack.

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices:
- SSL/TLS encryption
- Secure database storage
- Regular security audits
- SOC 2 compliance

### Who can see participant data?

Only workspace admins can access full participant data. Participants can only see their own information.

### Can I export my data?

Yes, admins can export all workspace data as CSV or JSON files from the Settings page.

## Technical

### What browsers are supported?

Changemaker works best on:
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

### Is there a mobile app?

Not currently, but our web platform is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers.

### Can I integrate with other tools?

Yes! We offer:
- REST API for custom integrations
- Webhooks for real-time events
- Official SDKs for popular languages

### Where can I find API documentation?

Visit our [API Reference](/docs/api-reference) for complete documentation.

## Billing

### How does billing work?

You're billed monthly based on your plan. Charges include:
- Platform subscription
- Active participants
- API usage (if applicable)

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your workspace will remain active until the end of your billing period.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and can arrange invoicing for enterprise customers.
