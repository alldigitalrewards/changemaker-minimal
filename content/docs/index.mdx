# Welcome to Changemaker

Changemaker is a comprehensive platform for managing innovation challenges, rewards, and participant engagement. Built for organizations that want to drive innovation and recognize contributors.

## What is Changemaker?

Changemaker enables organizations to:

- **Create Challenges**: Design and launch innovation challenges with custom requirements
- **Manage Participants**: Engage with participants and track their progress
- **Distribute Rewards**: Integrate with RewardStack to incentivize innovation
- **Track Analytics**: Monitor engagement and measure impact

## Key Features

### Multi-Tenant Workspaces

Each workspace is an isolated environment with its own:
- Challenges and participants
- Custom branding and settings
- Independent reward configurations
- Role-based access control

### Role-Based Access

Changemaker supports two primary roles:

- **ADMIN**: Full workspace management and configuration
- **PARTICIPANT**: Challenge enrollment and submission

### RewardStack Integration

Seamlessly distribute rewards through our RewardStack integration:
- Sync available SKUs automatically
- Issue rewards based on challenge completion
- Track reward distribution and status
