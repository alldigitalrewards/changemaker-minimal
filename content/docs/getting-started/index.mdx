# Getting Started with Changemaker

This guide will walk you through setting up your first workspace, creating a challenge, and inviting participants.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Changemaker account
- Admin access to create workspaces
- Basic understanding of challenge-based programs

## Step 1: Create a Workspace

Workspaces are isolated environments for managing challenges. To create your first workspace:

1. Navigate to the **Admin Dashboard**
2. Click **Create Workspace**
3. Enter your workspace details:
   - **Name**: Your organization or program name
   - **Slug**: URL-friendly identifier (e.g., `my-company`)
4. Click **Create**

Your workspace will be accessible at `/w/your-slug`.

## Step 2: Configure Workspace Settings

After creating your workspace, configure essential settings:

### Branding

Customize your workspace appearance:
- Upload your logo
- Set primary and secondary colors
- Add a welcome message for participants

### Integrations

Connect external services:
- **RewardStack**: Configure API credentials for reward distribution
- **Email**: Set up email templates for notifications

### Roles and Permissions

Manage team access:
- Invite admins to help manage challenges
- Set up participant enrollment settings

## Step 3: Create Your First Challenge

Now you're ready to create a challenge:

1. In your workspace, navigate to **Admin > Challenges**
2. Click **Create Challenge**
3. Fill in the challenge details:
   - **Title**: Clear, engaging name
   - **Description**: Explain goals and requirements
   - **Timeline**: Set start and end dates
   - **Rewards**: Configure reward distribution

4. Click **Publish** when ready

## Step 4: Invite Participants

Get your team involved:

### Email Invitations

Send direct invitations:
1. Go to **Admin > Participants**
2. Click **Invite Participants**
3. Enter email addresses
4. Customize the invitation message

### Public Enrollment

Allow open enrollment:
1. Navigate to **Challenge Settings**
2. Enable **Public Enrollment**
3. Share the enrollment link

## Step 5: Monitor Progress

Track challenge activity:

- **Dashboard**: View real-time engagement metrics
- **Submissions**: Review and approve participant submissions
- **Analytics**: Analyze participation patterns

## Common Questions

### How do I add more admins?

Navigate to **Workspace Settings > Team** and invite additional administrators.

### Can participants join multiple challenges?

Yes! Participants can enroll in as many challenges as they wish within a workspace.

### How are rewards distributed?

Rewards are distributed through RewardStack based on challenge completion criteria you define.
